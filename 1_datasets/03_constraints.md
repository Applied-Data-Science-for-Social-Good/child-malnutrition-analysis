# Data Constraints and Limitations

## Missing Data Patterns

### Geographic Gaps

- Low-income countries have more missing observations
- Conflict-affected regions often lack data
- Small island nations may be excluded

### Temporal Gaps

- UNICEF JME: Irregular updates, some countries go 5+ years without new data
- World Bank Education: Updated every 3-5 years for many countries
- Economic indicators: Generally more complete

## Known Limitations

### Measurement Issues

1. **Malnutrition indicators**: Based on surveys, not continuous monitoring
2. **GDP per capita**: Does not capture within-country inequality
3. **Refugee data**: May underestimate internal displacement
